Synergist is a hard rock band from Toronto, Canada. Their blend of alternative, metal and punk influences allows the band to put a focus on songwriting, while fulfiling a desire to challenge themselves musically at the same time. Steadily building a repertoire of songs and a strong band dynamic since 2006, Synergist utilize each member's skills and tastes for a melodic heavy sound.
 
The band has adopted the DIY ethic of many independant artists in this era, writing and recording material themselves. Fans can download Synergist's self-titled debut album from synergist.bandcamp.com.
