Synergist is a hard rock band from Toronto, Canada. Their blend of rock, metal and punk influences allows the band to put a focus on songwriting, while fulfiling a desire to challenge themselves musically at the same time. Steadily building a repertoire of songs and a strong band dynamic since 2006, Synergist utilize each member's skills and tastes for a melodic heavy sound.
 
The band has adopted the DIY ethic of many independant artists in this era, writing and recording material themselves. Fans can download their first release, 2008's "the Catalyst demos" online for free. New material can streamed above.
 
Synergist are currently hard at work preparing their self titled debut album, to be released sometime in 2013.
